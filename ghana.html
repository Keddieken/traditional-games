Traditional Games of Ghana

CHASKELE

Ghana

Kyaskele is a traditional Ghanaian game similar to dodgeball, played with a ball and involving quick reflexes and teamwork. Children form two teams and try to hit opponents with the ball while avoiding being hit themselves.

Kwasiada Frankaa

Ghana

Kwasiada Frankaa is a drama-like game involving songs, role-play, and movement. One child acts as the "flag" and others mimic or interpret scenes and characters, often from everyday life or moral stories. The game usually starts with a call-and-response song, and then the participants take turns performing or acting out various characters while the rest observe or join in.

Antoakyire

Ghana

A traditional Ghanaian game that involves teamwork, strategy, and quick thinking, often played by children in open spaces.

Sansankroma

Ghana

A popular Ghanaian children's game involving singing, clapping, and rhythm, often played in groups to build coordination and teamwork.
