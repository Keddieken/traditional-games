Traditional Games of South Africa

Diketo

South Africa

A traditional South African game involving stones, testing hand-eye coordination and speed.

Kgati

South Africa

A traditional South African skipping game played with a long rope, usually by girls in groups, to develop rhythm, coordination, and fitness.

Morabaraba

South Africa

A traditional South African board game of strategy, also known as "Twelve Men's Morris", played with 12 tokens per player on a special board. Players aim to form mills and capture opponent's pieces.

Ncuva

South Africa

A traditional South African board game from the Mancala family, played with pebbles and holes in the ground. Players move pieces to capture the opponent's stones using strategy and planning.
